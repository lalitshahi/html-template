About Center

“Caring for seniors is a labor of love and requires a special person”

Our Assisted Living program is designed to help you live a vibrant life by connecting with the people and community around you. In addition to the usual meals, activities and maintenance, residents can also get any combination of support services, like medication management, Diabetes management, help with bathing and dressing, tube feeding and incontinence management.

Acme assisted living offers affordable personal and health care services 24 hours a day, 365 days a year for those who need it.

You’ll work with a care coordinator at your local office to set a care schedule.

How We Work

“Caring for seniors is a labor of love and requires a special person”

Elderly care, or simply eldercare (also known as aged care), is the fulfillment of the special needs and requirements that are unique to senior citizens. This broad term encompasses such services as assisted living, adult day care, long-term care, nursing homes, hospice care, and our home like senior care.

Because of the wide variety of elderly care found nationally, as well as differentiating regional perspectives on elderly citizens, it cannot be limited to any single practice.
